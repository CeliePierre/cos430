1. What does it mean to say "Pet inherits from Dog"?  
Its means that the Pet class is a subclass of the Dog class, 
and therefore it inherits all the properties and methods of 
the Dog class. This means that any object created from 
the Pet class can use the properties and methods defined in
the Dog class, in addition to any properties and

2. Why does Dog depend on Date, but Pet and Competitor don't?
The Dog class depends on the Date class because it has a property
called "birthdate" which is of type Date. 
In contrast, Pet and Competitor do not have any properties 
that depend on the Date class, so they do not need to import it.

   properties of Competitor?
It's possible that the parameters in the Competitor constructor 
that are not properties of Competitor are being used to
initialize properties of a superclass that Competitor inherits from.

the constructor that are not stored as properties of the Competitor class.
